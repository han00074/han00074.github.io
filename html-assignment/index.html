About me

My name is Jialin Han. I am a level 1 interactive media design student. I come from China, but I have been in Canada for almost 9 years. Currently I live in Ottawa. I graduated from Moncton flight college with a diploma in aviation technology in April 25th 2015. After that I worked as a flight instructor in a flight training school, then worked as a pilot for general aviation company for 6 years.

During the COVID-19 pandemic I saw that all the people in world had a hard time for almost 2 years. Those years let me knew one thing - I should not put all my eggs in one basket. There are so many unpredictable things in life. That is why I quit my job and enrolled in the Algonquin College IMD program.

As a mature student I already know that going back to school is not that easy, especially when I do not have any relevant experience. But the subject is something that I like, so I feel it is worth working hard for.
